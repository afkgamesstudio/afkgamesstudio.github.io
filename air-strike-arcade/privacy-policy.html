Privacy Policy

Air Strike Arcade | Effective: Feb 14, 2026 | Updated: Feb 14, 2026

1. Corporate Declaration and Entity Scope

This Privacy Policy governs the data practices of AFK GAMES STUDIO (“the Studio,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) regarding the mobile application Air Strike Arcade (the “App”). The Studio operates under a strict Data Minimization Protocol. We do not require account registration, and we do not directly collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as legal names, physical addresses, or contact credentials.

2. Data Segregation and Local Storage (Offline-First)

The App is engineered as a primary offline experience. To facilitate core functionality, specific telemetry is stored exclusively on the user’s device via SharedPreferences and is not transmitted to Studio-operated servers. This data includes:

  • Game State: Local progress, combat statistics, and user preferences (Haptics, Music, SFX).
  • ZERO Protocol: AI/CPU tactical learning telemetry. This data is non-biometric, processed locally, and intended solely for enhancing CPU difficulty through internal algorithmic adjustments.

Users maintain absolute control over this data and may execute a permanent erasure at any time via the in-game “Wipe Memory” function.

3. Automated Third-Party Processing

The App integrates Enterprise SDKs to facilitate monetization and diagnostics. These partners (Google AdMob and Google Play Services) may automatically ingest:

  • Persistent Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID), IP addresses (for approximate location), and device metadata.
  • Technical Telemetry: Hardware specifications, Android OS version, and runtime diagnostics.

Users may exercise their right to limit tracking via Android System Settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

4. Synthetic Media and Audio Asset Disclosure

The App incorporates assets generated via advanced AI technologies and licensed libraries:

  • Suno AI: Musical compositions generated under a valid commercial license held by AFK GAMES STUDIO.
  • Pixabay: Sound effects (SFX) utilized under the Pixabay Content License.

These technologies do not collect user telemetry through the App interface.

5. Global Regulatory Compliance

  • COPPA (US): The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.
  • GDPR (EEA/UK): Processing of technical data is based on our Legitimate Interest. Consent for advertising is managed via the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).
  • CCPA (California): We do not “sell” personal information. Data sharing is limited to defined business purposes.

6. Data Security and Integrity

We utilize commercially acceptable security protocols to protect the integrity of processed telemetry. However, given the nature of electronic transmission, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Data Retention Policy

Local game telemetry is retained indefinitely until manual erasure by the user or App uninstallation. Third-party advertising data is retained by Google according to their statutory retention cycles (typically 14–26 months).

8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Florida, USA. Any disputes arising from these data practices shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in Orange County, Florida.
