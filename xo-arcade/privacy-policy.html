Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

1. Corporate Declaration

AFK GAMES STUDIO ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile game XO Arcade: Infinity Edition (the "Service"). We adhere to a strict Data Minimization Protocol; we do not require user account registration and do not directly collect, store, or solicit Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as legal names, physical addresses, or contact credentials.

2. Local Data Processing (ZERO Protocol)

The Service is engineered as a primary offline experience. To facilitate core gameplay, specific telemetry is stored exclusively on the user’s device and is not transmitted to Studio-operated servers. This includes:

  • Game State: Local progress, settings, and gameplay statistics.
  • ZERO Protocol: AI/CPU learning telemetry. This data is non-biometric, processed locally, and intended solely for enhancing CPU difficulty through internal algorithmic adjustments.

3. Automated Third-Party Processing

To facilitate monetization and diagnostics, the Service utilizes Google AdMob and Google Play Services. These partners may automatically ingest:

  • Persistent Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID), IP addresses, and device metadata.
  • Technical Telemetry: Hardware specifications, Android OS version, and runtime diagnostics.

Users may exercise their right to limit tracking via Android System Settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

4. Synthetic Media Disclosure

The Service incorporates assets generated via advanced AI technologies, including ElevenLabs (voice synthesis) and Suno AI (musical compositions). These assets are utilized under valid commercial licenses held by AFK GAMES STUDIO. These technologies do not collect user telemetry through the Service interface.

5. Global Regulatory Compliance
